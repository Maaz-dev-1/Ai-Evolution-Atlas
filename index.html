What is Artificial Intelligence?

The science of creating intelligent machines that can think, learn, and adapt

Artificial Intelligence represents humanity's quest to replicate and enhance human cognitive abilities through computational systems. From simple rule-based programs to complex neural networks, AI has evolved to become an integral part of our digital landscape.

The Evolution Timeline

Key milestones in AI development

1956

Dartmouth Conference

The birth of AI as a field. The term "Artificial Intelligence" was coined at the Dartmouth Conference.

1969

First Robot

Shakey the robot becomes the first mobile robot with AI capabilities at Stanford Research Institute.

1997

Deep Blue Wins

IBM's Deep Blue defeats world chess champion Garry Kasparov, marking a major AI milestone.

2011

Watson on Jeopardy

IBM Watson wins Jeopardy! against human champions, demonstrating natural language processing.

2016

AlphaGo Victory

Google's AlphaGo defeats world champion Lee Sedol in the complex game of Go.

2022

ChatGPT Era

OpenAI's ChatGPT launches, bringing AI to mainstream consciousness and daily use.

2030+
